Our Story

Making the Old New Again

I’ve been collecting vintage charms for as long as I can remember. There’s something magical about a tiny gold charm that has been worn, loved, and passed down through generations — each one carries its own silent history.

Running True Vintage Jewellery lets me share that love with a new generation. I hand-pick every single piece — only genuine vintage 9ct gold and sterling silver — and love helping customers build charm bracelets they’ll treasure for years to come. Based in London, shipping worldwide.
